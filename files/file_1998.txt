                                        60612 Cost FREE Calendar Download iCal File Every August, prior to the start of the Fall Semester, the Graduate College sponsors a mandatory Campus-Wide New Teaching Assistant (TA) Orientation.  All graduate students who will be TAs at UIC for the first time during the upcoming Fall and/or Spring Semester are required to attend.  The purpose of the orientation is to provide new TAs with information on the relevant policies, procedures, and campus resources that will help them successfully fulfill their TA duties.
