PGA Mission To Uphold and Enhance the Reputation, Image and Values of UIC and to Showcase and Celebrate UIC’s Diverse and Inclusive Community. The Office of Public and Government Affairs is focused on institutional advocacy to establish strong relationships with outside constituencies and build broad understanding of UIC’s contributions locally, nationally, and worldwide. Units within PGA are responsible for developing and broadcasting strategic communications and supporting campus units by providing best practices and resources to ensure that marketing and communications are aligned with UIC’s brand and identity.
