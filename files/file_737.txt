Through diverse perspectives from our interdisciplinary cohort of graduate students and faculty (Engineering, Medicine, Computer Science, Design, and Business), MAD Lab conducts clinical needs validation, generates innovative concept solutions, and provides a thorough outline of the potential market opportunity.
