LINK Virtual Reality Undergraduate Project Laboratories About News Members Projects Feedback Contact Links About The Virtual Reality Undergraduate Projects Laboratory, 
									( VRUPL ) is an environment where promising and 
								enthusiastic undergraduate students can learn about virtual reality through 
								participation in a wide variety of VR related projects. These projects include 
								research-oriented activities in support of funded faculty research projects as 
								well as some projects that are just plain fun and educational. ( VRUPL is 
								a satellite lab of the world renowned Electronic 
									Visualization Laboratory , developers of the CAVE. ) Just like the VRUPL lab itself, this web site is still in the early 
								stages of development, and will hopefully contain more information as the lab 
								grows and prospers.
