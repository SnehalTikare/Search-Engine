Material Transfer Agreements (MTA) refer to an agreement in which a University lab either sends or receives tangible research materials to another lab or entity.   Tangible research materials include, but are not limited to:  plasmids, antibodies, cell stocks, cultures, animal models, prototypes, chemical compounds, and equipment.   For more information about initiating an MTA, contact the Office of Research Services here: Material Transfer Agreements Sponsored Research Agreements (SRA) refer to an agreement between UIC researchers and an outside entity which is offering to fund research towards a specific goal.  Any proposal must be routed through the Office of Research Services.  More information may be found under the Sponsored Programs section of the OVCR website here: Preparing a Proposal Call to Action Edit icon Submit Your Idea!
