Student Assistance staff are available to consult with students, faculty, staff, families and community members/providers to determine the best approach for a student’s current situation. Life events can interfere with a student’s ability to be successful at UIC. When a challenging situation arises, a student or family may find it difficult to navigate the many resources and services that are available at the institution.
