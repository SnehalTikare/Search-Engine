Graduation Rates – Provides six-year graduation rate of certificate or degree-seeking, first-time, full-time undergraduate students. Graduation Rates for Students Receiving Athletically Related Student Aid (NCAA) – Provides information for degree-seeking first-time, full-time undergraduates who received athletically related student aid. Post-Graduation Information – The institution does not calculate placement rates or advertise job placement rates as a means of recruiting students. Additionally, it does not advertise the types of graduate and professional education in which graduates of the institution's 4-year degree programs enroll.
