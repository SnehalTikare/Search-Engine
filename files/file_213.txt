Research Interests: Computational fluid dynamics Turbulence Direct numerical simulation Filtered mass density function Particle tracking algorithms Combustion Parallelization and high-performance computing Spray and atomization Selected Grants US Army, Rapid Additive Manufacturing of Electrically Integrated Drone Arms, Principal Investigator US Army, Route the Gap: A Semi-Autonomous Wet Gap Crossing, Principal Investigator Publication Aggregators Google Scholar ResearchGate Service to Community Jonathan Komperda serves as the administrator of the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering's computational clusters Dragon and Hydra.
