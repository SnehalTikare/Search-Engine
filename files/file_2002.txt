The Innovation Center is readily accessible by public transportation as well as by car. The Eisenhower Expressway (I-290) passes one block north of the UIC Innovation Center. Directions By Car: (From North): Take I-94E exit 51H, merge onto Eisenhower Expy/I-290W.
