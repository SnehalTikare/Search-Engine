The IGERT curriculum is tailored individually to every student, based on their research projects and interests. The curriculum includes topics in cybersecurity, communication, public policy, and information and decision sciences, and is decided upon acceptance to the program in coordination with the IGERT Fellows, their PhD advisors in their home department, and the IGERT Executive Committee.
