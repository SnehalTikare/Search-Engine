Established in 2001, the board is composed of outstanding professionals who represent a broad range of industrial firms. Professor Sabri Cetin , the board’s faculty coordinator, works with Department Head Farzad Mashayek to facilitate the board’s role in shaping the way in which UIC educates future mechanical and industrial engineers.
